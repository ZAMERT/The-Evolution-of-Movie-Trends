# The Evolution of Movie Trends

## Overview

This project is aimed at analyzing the relationship between movie genres, audience preferences, and streaming platforms' choices. By analyzing random movies data from IMDB, Amazon, Disney+, and Netflix, we aim to understand how movies trends have evolved over time.

In the following part of the project, we deal with the chosen datasets with data manipulation and visualization tools. We will further analyze the data with the application of machine learning techniques in the following months, in order to find the evolution of movie trends in the industry.

----------

## Motivation

Movies are one of the essentials in our daily life, and watching movies is a very popular form of entertainment and relaxation. Since 28 December 1895, when the Lumiere brothers' had held the first commercial, public screening of ten short films in Paris (Wikipedia, History of film), the film industry has achieved significant growth worldwide. Over the past 130 years, with the vigorous development of technology, the way people watched movies has also been constantly changing, which in turn has influenced the production, distribution and consumption of films. As a result, various genres of movies have emerged throughout the history, and the entire industry has flourished. <br>

In recent decades, the film industry has undergone another major transformation (Wikipedia, History of Film): a rapid shift toward streaming platforms such as Amazon, Netflix, and Disney+. These streaming platforms, together with the cinemas, now represent two dominant modes of film distribution, which also bring new challenges and opportunities for the future development of the film industry. <br>
<br>

We hope that this model can be used to predict the genres of movies that will be popular in the future.<br>
